# App Review Reply

Thank you for the follow-up. Please see the detailed answers below.

1. Does the app collect user or usage data?

The app does not create user accounts and does not collect data for tracking users across apps or websites.

The app does send user-triggered search requests to SAM.gov when the user performs an opportunity search.

The app also uses Google AdMob in non-personalized mode only. In that configuration, Google AdMob may process limited device, diagnostic, and usage data needed to load ads, prevent fraud, and measure ad delivery. The app does not use this data for cross-app or cross-website tracking.

2. Where can the user trigger AppTrackingTransparency in the app?

There is no AppTrackingTransparency prompt in the app because AppTrackingTransparency was removed from this build. The app does not request permission to track because it does not track users across apps or websites.

3. Does the privacy policy reflect the current behavior?

Yes. The privacy policy was updated to reflect the current non-tracking implementation, including:
- no AppTrackingTransparency prompt
- non-personalized ads only
- no cross-app or cross-website tracking
- SAM.gov search requests and local on-device storage

The privacy policy can be opened from:
- Settings > Ad Privacy > Open Privacy Policy
- Settings > Support > Open Privacy Policy

The policy URL used by the app points to the current submission branch version of the privacy policy in the repository.
